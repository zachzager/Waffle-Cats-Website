Features

  1. Eight playable cats
  2. Three different stages
  3. Local AND network multiplayer (coming soon)
  4. Beautiful art
  5. Catchy music

The Inspiration

While we've been influenced by one button games like Flappy Bird and Tiny Wings, Cheese Dash Ninja is our biggest source of inspiration. Addictinggames.com cites Maksim Eliseenko as the developer, though his website does not corroborate this. We were inspired by the basic one-button controls and the bouncing mechanic.
